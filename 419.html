MIS

Management Information System (MIS) is a comprehensive and organized framework of technologies, processes, and personnel designed to gather, process, store, and disseminate information within an organization. MIS plays a pivotal role in supporting decision-making processes at various levels of management by providing timely, accurate, and relevant information.
